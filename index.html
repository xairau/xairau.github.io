How did I get into streetwear fashion?
I got into streetwear fashion because of school and social media. I would see the way certain people dressed, and I liked it. I also
discovered it by listening to certain artists like A$AP Rocky, Lil Uzi Vert, and Famous Dex. This style that they had has
influenced the things I like from the way I dressed, to the brands that I liked.
What is Streetwear?
Streetwear is a fashion style that is most commonly associated with rappers. Many brands have inspired the streetwear fashion
of today. For example, brands like Supreme, BAPE (A Bathing Ape), Stussy, Anti Social Social Club, and Off-White. Sneaker brands
like Jordan and Yeezy have also become a major staple of streetwear fashion. It is commonly incorporated into streetwear outfits.
